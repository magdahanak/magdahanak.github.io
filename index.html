Graphic Designer

As a forward-looking, optimistic and open person, I enjoy creating new things and applying innovative methods in my designs. I believe that each design must make you happy, as well as be unique, elegant, and neat, regardless it is for a business or personal use. I see graphic design along with photography, which is my hobby, as a way of expressing myself and co-creating the world we live in.

At the moment, I am based in Bedford, UK. Yet, I would love to work with and for you, making your unique designs come true, regardless of your location. Why Forest&Wine - behind the scenes

Forest is one of my favourite places on Earth. I love it because of the silence that allows me to hear every beat of my heart. I love listening the stories carried by the wind in branches of the trees. This is the place where my mind is completely clear from the city noise. There I get new ideas and find inspiration for every special project!

Wine is full of colours, aroma, and taste. Every bottle brings you something different – something that you will fancy. The beauty of colours, richness of aromas, and tenderness of taste make me feel comfortable and happy. Wine tell the stories too – the stories of its life from a seed to a bottle. Each such story makes me happy – I feel inspired and ready to create something new and fresh.

Forest&Wine Shop is part of my portfolio – you can order something directly from there! However, this is not the only way you can order my designs. I am happy to create new designs that will be customised or personalised to suit your needs.
